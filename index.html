Introduction

This is a website dedicated to highlighting some really interesting up and coming artists across multiple different music genres. The goal is to highlight the works of smaller artists and expose you to music that isnt as mainstream to allow people to broaden their spectrum of music taste and find new artists for their playlists. Feel free to suggest an artist you think is worthy of recognition in the suggest section also.

Featured Artist

Birds By Saoirse Miller

Saoirse Miller

Saoirse Miller is an independent composer, producer and singer-songwriter from Dublin. Drawing inspiration from Irish folklore, pagan mysticism and hauntology, Saoirse's music blends natural sounds with electronics and her otherworldly vocals, to create ethereal electro-acoustic ambience.
