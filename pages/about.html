Site Purpose

Next-Up Music was created to spotlight underrated and emerging artists across diverse genres — from indie and jazz to cinematic and cultural sounds. We aim to help music enthusiasts discover new voices that deserve more recognition.

Who Is This Site For?

This site is for listeners who want to explore beyond mainstream charts — people curious about fresh sounds, untold stories, and experimental genres.

Project Background

This site was built as part of a UCD web design project, with a focus on accessible, responsive, and user-centered design.
